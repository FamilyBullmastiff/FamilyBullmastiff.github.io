If you want...

  • a bullmastiff that is flexible around anything and everything...
  • a bullmastiff that will be a joy to the whole family, both indoors and out...
  • a bullmastiff of excellent conformation and storied bloodlines, but recognize that doing genetic and multi-organ specialist testing is mostly a money sink and brings a false sense of security...

Then welcome to Buckeye Bullmastiffs

Located in Dayton Ohio, our dogs are OFA hip certified and come from pedigrees that do not have characteristic genetic trends that produce subpar results. We breed for correct conformation, excellent health, exceptional temperament, and the ease and flexibility of adjusting to a wide range of environments and interactions. In order to achieve these goals and maintain our high standards, we purposefully limit ourselves to remain a small, family-oriented, and family-run breeder. Details concerning our most recent litter can be found in the Current Litter section.
